## Procurement & Institutional Channel

Exploring WA government/institutional furniture procurement as a future
channel:

- **CUAFWE2024** — WA's Furniture, White Goods and Electrical Appliances
  Common Use Agreement. Non-mandatory. 2024 update added a 10-year warranty
  requirement on office/classroom furniture.
- **IGS Group** (igsgroup.com.au) — strongest identified fit for BIM content
  distribution. Australian, runs BIMcontent.com, used by tier-1 ANZ studios
  (Woods Bagot, Hassell, Cox, Bates Smart). Publishes a public Revit Content
  Standard: Revit 2025 base, underscore-separated Title Case naming,
  mandatory IGS Shared Parameters file, size caps (600KB simple / 1MB
  complex family).
- **Planned pipeline:** Fusion 360 → SAT export → Revit family (RFA),
  authored to IGS's standard. Sequence: get the standard PDF → author one
  test family → submit for QA → only then commit to the full ~15-piece
  catalogue.
